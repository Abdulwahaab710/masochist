---
title: Synergy 3.1 now available
---

After a successful [beta-testing cycle](http://lists.wincent.com/pipermail/synergy-beta/2006-November/000003.html) the final release version of [Synergy](http://synergy.wincent.com/) 3.1 is now available for immediate download. This is a feature-oriented release which brings [Audioscrobbler support](http://www.wincent.com/a/products/synergy-classic/features/audioscrobbler/). A full changelog of enhancements, bugfixes and other changes appears [here](http://www.wincent.com/a/products/synergy-classic/history/#3.1).

You can download the new release [from here](http://www.wincent.com/download.php?item=Synergy.dmg) or purchase a license using [this page](https://secure.wincent.com/a/products/synergy-classic/purchase/).
