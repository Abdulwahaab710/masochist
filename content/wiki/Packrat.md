A [packrat parser](/wiki/packrat_parser) is a memoizing parser that recognizes [Parsing Expression Grammars](/wiki/Parsing_Expression_Grammars).

My [object-oriented templating](/wiki/object-oriented_templating) system, [Walrus](/wiki/Walrus), includes a [packrat parser](/wiki/packrat_parser). The generator itself (written in [Ruby](/wiki/Ruby)) is available in a separate project, [Walrat](/wiki/Walrat).
