[MediaWiki](/wiki/MediaWiki) is the powerful, scalable and extremely popular [wiki](/wiki/wiki) software used to power [Wikipedia](/wiki/Wikipedia) and a great many other wikis on the Internet. I myself used it for over a year to host my "[Knowledge base](/wiki/Knowledge_base)", before replacing it with the custom wiki that you're reading now.
