[Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5](/wiki/Red_Hat_Enterprise_Linux_5) or simply [RHEL 5](/wiki/RHEL_5) was released in March 2007, and will be supported by [Red Hat](/wiki/Red_Hat) in one way or another until 2014 (see "[Red Hat Enterprise Linux support policy (EOL dates)](/wiki/Red_Hat_Enterprise_Linux_support_policy_%28EOL_dates%29)").

I wasn't able to find an "official" list of installed software packages with [RHEL 5](/wiki/RHEL_5) but I did find [this listing](http://ftp.redhat.com/pub/redhat/linux/enterprise/5Server/en/os/SRPMS/) of [RPMs](/wiki/RPMs), which indicates that the base versions of the following packages are included:

-   [Apache](/wiki/Apache) 2.2.3
-   [MySQL](/wiki/MySQL) 5.0.22
-   [PHP](/wiki/PHP) 5.1.6
-   [Ruby](/wiki/Ruby)Â 1.8.5
-   [Python](/wiki/Python) 2.4.3
-   [Perl](/wiki/Perl) 5.8.8
-   [GCC](/wiki/GCC) 4.1.1
-   [Subversion](/wiki/Subversion) 1.4.2
-   [Mailman](/wiki/Mailman) 2.1.9
-   [Sendmail](/wiki/Sendmail) 8.13.8
-   [procmail](/wiki/procmail) 3.22
-   [Squirrelmail](/wiki/Squirrelmail) 1.4.8
-   [SpamAssassin](/wiki/SpamAssassin) 3.1.7/3.1.8/3.1.9
