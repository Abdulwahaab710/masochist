[ZFS](/wiki/ZFS) or the [Zettabyte File System](/wiki/Zettabyte_File_System) is a revolutionary file-system made by [Sun Microsystems](/wiki/Sun_Microsystems) and ported by [Apple](/wiki/Apple) to [Mac OS X](/wiki/Mac_OS_X) for [Leopard](/wiki/Leopard). At the time of writing, it has never enjoyed "official" status as a supported read-write file system for Mac OS X, and the official [project page](http://zfs.macosforge.org/) indicated that the project was discontinued in October 2009. [Speculation](http://www.reddit.com/r/programming/comments/9x4sb/zfs_project_on_macosforgecom_officially/) indicates that this might have occurred due to licensing difficulties.
