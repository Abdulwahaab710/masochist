[Objective-C 2.0](/wiki/Objective-C_2.0) is the new version of [Objective-C](/wiki/Objective-C) that ships with [Mac OS X](/wiki/Mac_OS_X) [Leopard](/wiki/Leopard). Highights include:

-   Garbage collection
-   Properties
-   Fast enumeration (`for`/`in` syntax)
