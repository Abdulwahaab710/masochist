[Middleman](/wiki/Middleman) is a static site generator written in Ruby. It follows patterns that will be familiar to users of [Rails](/wiki/Rails) or [Sinatra](/wiki/Sinatra) (such as layouts, helpers) and integrates nicely with tools like [Haml](/wiki/Haml), [Sass](/wiki/Sass) and [CoffeeScript](/wiki/CoffeeScript).
