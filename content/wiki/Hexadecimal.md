[Hexadecimal](/wiki/Hexadecimal) or [hex](/wiki/hex) numbers are integers written in [base-16](/wiki/base-16). The possible range for each digit is from 0 through to 9 then a through f (inclusive). That is "a" in [hexadecimal](/wiki/hexadecimal) is equivalent to 10 in [decimal](/wiki/decimal).

You can use a tool like [WinHex](/wiki/WinHex) to perform conversions between numbers of different bases.
