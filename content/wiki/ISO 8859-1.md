[ISO 8859-1](/wiki/ISO_8859-1) or [Latin-1](/wiki/Latin-1) is a popular 8-bit text encoding.

It is the default encoding for [MySQL](/wiki/MySQL) databases (see "[Finding out the encoding of a MySQL database](/wiki/Finding_out_the_encoding_of_a_MySQL_database)" and "[Setting the encoding of a MySQL database](/wiki/Setting_the_encoding_of_a_MySQL_database)").
