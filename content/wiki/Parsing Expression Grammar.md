A [Parsing Expression Grammar](/wiki/Parsing_Expression_Grammar) is a non-ambiguous grammar useful for describing computer language syntax.

My [object-oriented templating](/wiki/object-oriented_templating) system, [Walrus](/wiki/Walrus), uses a parser generator that creates parsers from [Parsing Expression Grammars](/wiki/Parsing_Expression_Grammars). The generator itself (written in [Ruby](/wiki/Ruby)) is available in the [Walrat](/wiki/Walrat) project.
