[RFC 1630](/wiki/RFC_1630), or "Universal Resource Identifiers in WWW", is an [RFC](/wiki/RFC) proposing the formalization of a distinction between [URLs](/wiki/URLs), [URIs](/wiki/URIs) and [URNs](/wiki/URNs).
