[RFC 822](/wiki/RFC_822) is an [RFC](/wiki/RFC) describing "STANDARD FOR THE FORMAT OF ARPA INTERNET TEXT MESSAGES". It was later updated and superceded by [RFC 2822](/wiki/RFC_2822).
