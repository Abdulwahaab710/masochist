[Usenet](/wiki/Usenet) is a distributed discussion network organized into hierarchically-ordered [newsgroups](/wiki/newsgroups) and accessed via the [NNTP](/wiki/NNTP) protocol or using a [web](/wiki/web) interface such as that provided by [Google](/wiki/Google) at <http://groups.google.com/>.
