[Spamhaus](/wiki/Spamhaus) is one of the most reputable and accurate [DNS-based blacklists](/wiki/DNS-based_blacklists) used to [combat spam](/wiki/combat_spam).

Their [SBL Advisory](http://www.spamhaus.org/sbl/index.lasso) is "free for individuals operating small mail servers as long as your email traffic is low". Their [XBL advisory](http://www.spamhaus.org/xbl/index.lasso) is also free for users with "normal mail servers". [Paid yearly subscriptions](http://www.spamhaus.org/datafeed/index.html) are available for other users not falling into these categories.
