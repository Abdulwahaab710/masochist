A [MTA](/wiki/MTA) or [Mail Transfer Agent](/wiki/Mail_Transfer_Agent) (sometimes also [Mail Transport Agent](/wiki/Mail_Transport_Agent)) is a program that transfers [mail](/wiki/mail) from one computer to another over a network using a protocol like [SMTP](/wiki/SMTP). [Sendmail](/wiki/Sendmail) is an example of a popular MTA.
