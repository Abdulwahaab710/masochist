[tmux](/wiki/tmux) is a [BSD](/wiki/BSD)-licensed [terminal](/wiki/terminal) multiplexer much like [GNU](/wiki/GNU) [screen](/wiki/screen), but, in my opinion, better documented and easier to use. It is also under relatively active development, compared to screen.
