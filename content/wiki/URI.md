A [URI](/wiki/URI) or [Uniform Resource Identifier](/wiki/Uniform_Resource_Identifier) is a string that identifies a resource. A [URI](/wiki/URI) may be considered a [URL](/wiki/URL), a [URN](/wiki/URN), or both.
