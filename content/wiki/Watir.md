[Watir](/wiki/Watir) or "[Web Application Testing in Ruby](/wiki/Web_Application_Testing_in_Ruby)" is a high-level integration testing tool.

I use it to perform automated in-browser testing of [Rails](/wiki/Rails) applications. See "[Using Watir with RSpec and Rails](/wiki/Using_Watir_with_RSpec_and_Rails)" for more details.
