[WebKit](/wiki/WebKit) is the open-source browser engine developed by [Apple](/wiki/Apple) for use in its [Safari](/wiki/Safari) browser. It is an integral part of [Mac OS X](/wiki/Mac_OS_X) and is leveraged by many third-party applications including browsers like [OmniWeb](/wiki/OmniWeb) and many, many non-browser applications.
