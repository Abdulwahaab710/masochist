[Scriptaculous](/wiki/Scriptaculous) is an easy-to-use [JavaScript](/wiki/JavaScript) library for adding [AJAX](/wiki/AJAX) functionality to modern [web](/wiki/web) applications. Personally, I much prefer using an [Unobtrusive JavaScript](/wiki/Unobtrusive_JavaScript) approach with the aid of [jQuery](/wiki/jQuery).
