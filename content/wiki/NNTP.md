[NNTP](/wiki/NNTP), or the [Network News Transfer Protocol](/wiki/Network_News_Transfer_Protocol), is used for reading and posting articles to [Usenet](/wiki/Usenet). It is described in [RFC 977](/wiki/RFC_977), which was later superseded by [RFC 3977](/wiki/RFC_3977).
