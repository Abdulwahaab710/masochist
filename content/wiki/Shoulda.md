[Shoulda](/wiki/Shoulda) is a [BDD](/wiki/BDD) test framework for [Ruby](/wiki/Ruby) designed to run on top of [Test::Unit](/wiki/Test%3a%3aUnit). It can also be used on top of [RSpec](/wiki/RSpec).
