These notes were made while upgrading to [RubyInline 3.6.7](/wiki/RubyInline_3.6.7) on [Mac OS X](/wiki/Mac_OS_X) [Leopard](/wiki/Leopard) 10.5.2.

    sudo gem install RubyInline
