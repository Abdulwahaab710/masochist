[GNU](/wiki/GNU) is a recursive acronym which stands for [GNU's Not UNIX](/wiki/GNU%27s_Not_UNIX).

[GNU](/wiki/GNU) software includes [GCC](/wiki/GCC), [GDB](/wiki/GDB) and many, many other free and open source packages. Many of these packages are included with [Mac OS X](/wiki/Mac_OS_X).
